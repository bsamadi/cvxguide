# Convex Optimization

A mathematical optimization is convex if the objective is a convex function and
the feasible set is a convex set. The standard form of a convex optimization
problem is:
\begin{align*}
\text{minimize }   & f_0(x) \\
\text{subject to } & f_i(x) \leq 0,\ i=1,\ldots,m \\
                  & h_i(x) = 0,\ i=1,\ldots,p
\end{align*}
